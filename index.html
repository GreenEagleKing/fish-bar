About Us

Serving Fresh Fish Since 1989

At Magna Fish Bar, we know our fish – we’ve been sourcing only the finest and freshest for almost thirty years.

Our local, family-run restaurants offer traditional fish and chips alongside a changing menu of delicious grilled and seafood options – all served with a big, warm welcome.

This is classic British food at its best. Succulent fish wrapped in crispy batter. Fluffy, authentic ‘chip-shop’ chips. Mrs Davis' home-made pickles and cakes. Whether you eat in or take away, Magna Fish Bar is a friendly and relaxed dining experience where it’s the food that steals the show.
